FAQ

How does Janus select which words to translate?

Our AI analyzes the text's context, your learning level, and vocabulary frequency to choose the most beneficial words for your language learning journey.

Will this slow down my browsing?

Not at all! Janus is optimized for performance and works seamlessly in the background without affecting page load times or browsing speed.

Is my browsing data private?

Absolutely. We only process the minimal text needed for translations and never store your browsing history or personal information.

Can I use Janus on mobile?

Currently Janus works on desktop Chrome and Chromium-based browsers. Mobile support is coming soon!

What languages are supported?

We support over 30 languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and many more.
