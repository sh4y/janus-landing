Frequently Asked Questions

How is Premium different from the free version?
While the free version offers basic translation features, Premium removes all limits on translations, enables multi-language learning, adds personal vocabulary tracking, and provides enhanced customization options to perfectly match your learning style.
Can I change my target language(s)?
Yes! Premium members can change their target languages at any time from their dashboard. Polyglot and Linguist members can learn multiple languages simultaneously.
Will Premium slow down my browsing?
Not at all. Janus Premium is engineered for performance, with optimizations that ensure translations happen quickly without affecting page load times.
How do you select which words to translate?
Our AI-powered algorithm selects words based on your chosen difficulty level, vocabulary frequency in the language, and usefulness in everyday conversation. Premium members get access to advanced customization of these parameters.
Is my browsing data private?
Absolutely. Janus only processes the text needed for translations and never stores your browsing history or personal data.
