FAQ

How does Janus select which words to translate?

Our AI analyzes the text's context, your learning level, and vocabulary frequency to choose the most beneficial words for your language learning journey.

Will this slow down my browsing?

Not at all! Janus is optimized for performance and works seamlessly in the background without affecting page load times or browsing speed.

Is my browsing data private?

Absolutely. We only process the minimal text needed for translations and never store your browsing history or personal information. All data is routed and parsed exclusively through private servers hosted on Microsoft infrastructure, with strict agreements preventing anyone (including Microsoft) from viewing your data. Your privacy is our promise.

Can I use Janus on mobile?

Currently Janus works on desktop Chrome and Chromium-based browsers. Mobile support is coming soon!

What languages are supported?

We support over 30 languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and many more.

What if Janus gets too distracting or annoying?

We get it—sometimes you just want to focus or enjoy a website without any extra help. That's why Janus lets you pause itself for 15 minutes with a single click, or even deactivate it completely if you need a longer break. You're always in control, so you can decide when you want to learn and when you just want to browse. It's all about making language learning fit your life, not the other way around.
